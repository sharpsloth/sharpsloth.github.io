Napolean Dynamite Festival

by Summer Wheatly

This summer we are bringing back the Napolean Dynamite Festival! Jason Blazzard, winner of the last Tater Tot eating contest told us he is excited to face any and all new challengers. So warm up those ovens and practice with all the tots you can handle. Back again is the tetherball tournament, football throwing contest, and all the fun events you remember. We have more spots for vendors who would like to set up a booth, so please contact the committee if you are interested. Hope to see everybody there to celebrate this iconic movie that put Preston on the map!
